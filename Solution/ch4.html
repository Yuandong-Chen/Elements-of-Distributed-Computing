4.1 Explain why, in the proof of Lemma 4.4, the rank of a state s has been defined as the length of the longest chain from any initial state to s rather than the langth from the inital state in s.p to s.

Ans: In order to guarantee the following property:

a →k b ⇒ rank(a) < rank(b), where k >= 1

4.2 Specify and prove properties of the following algorithm:

For any inital state s:

    (∀i: i ≠s.p: s.v[i] = 0) and (s.v[s.p] = 1)

(s, send, t):
